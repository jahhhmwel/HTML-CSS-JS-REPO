HOW TO MAKE ADOBO


My version of the traditional Filipino dish. This can be prepped well ahead of time, leaving the pork to marinate for at least a couple of hours, the longer the better. Adobo is the name for the marinade.

Ingredients

  • ½ pound pork belly, cut into 1 1/2-inch cubes
  • ½ pound pork loin, cut into 1 1/2-inch cubes
  • 2 tablespoons groundnut oil, divided
  • 4 cloves garlic, diced

Steps

  1. Combine pork belly and pork loin in a large bowl.
  2. Toss with 1 tablespoon groundnut oil to coat.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, red chile, peppercorns, brown sugar, bay leaves, and cumin.
